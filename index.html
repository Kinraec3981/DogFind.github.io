Rules

  • Runs require full audio
  • You cannot use your own dog
  • The video of the run needs first 10 seconds of shown you started riding your bike and then the video can be black or not showing the track but when you find the dog breed for your run you have to do a picture of the dog and a full picture of an object which is in the background of the first picture of the dog. Lastly include you finding the dog.
  • The full run consists of: Video with first and last 10 second fo start and finsh of the run, Picture of the dog, Picture of an object in the dog's background.
  • We would like the pictures to be submitted by Imgur or Google Drive. The videos can be submitted by Google Drive or YouTube

Run Overview

  1. Get on your bike and say which dog breed are you looking for (Include it in video).
  2. Have your camera showing the whole situation and you riding out of your possesion. When you ride out of your house or garden if you see the dog breed from there it doesn't count.
  3. Then you can leave the camera or phone somewhere where the video doesnt need to be shown but the whole audio including: you breathing and riding the bike
  4. When you find the dog start showing the whole situation on camera (the dog's guardian face doesn't have ot be included). Ask the dog's guardian if you can take a picture of the dog and also remember to take a picture of one things in the dog's background to avoid cheating and finding the dog breed in the internet
  5. Submit: the dog breed you have chosen; your name or nickname you want see on the leaderboards; the time of the run and the country where the run was done to the given email address: dogfind.speedruns@gmail.com

Equipment Required

  • Bicycle
  • Camera, phone or any recording device with recording sound
  • Any photo taking device. Atleast 480p

Often Asked Questions

  • Q: Do I have to start from my house?
    A: Yes, you have to start from your house or any house to which you have permission to come into
  • Q: Can i keep the audio in a seperate file?
    A: No, you have to have only one full video of the run
  • Q: How long will it take for my run to be submitted?
    A: No more than 1 week. I've got also some other work to do. I will verify runs whenever it is possible :)
  • Q: What if my run gets rejected?
    A: I will write an email to you why the run was rejected so you can make it better next time.
  • Q: What if the dog's guardian rejects to take a picture of the dog?
    A: There is nothing we can do about this, sorry. Thy again for a new dog.
